Back from their little walk outside, the Bear's came back to their house expecting three bowls of warm porridge waiting for them. However, when they walked into their kitchen, all three noticed something wrong with their porridge bowls: their spoons were already in their bowls! And they had not touched them at all! Papa Bear, Mama Bear, and Baby Bear let out upset cries.

"Someone has been at my porridge!"

"Someone has been at my porridge!"

"Someone has been at my porridge, and has eaten it all up!!"

The three bears then moved to the living area, where they found out their chairs had been moved around the room from their original spots. The three bears let out more angry cries, for it was obvious someone had been scurrying around their house without their permission.

"Someone has been sitting in my chair!"

"Someone has been sitting in my chair!"

"Someone has been sitting in my chair, and they broke it!!"

Finally, the three bears moved anxiously upstairs to their bedroom, not knowing what to expect. Upon entering the bedroom, they looked around and realized the sheets on their beds had been pulled back. It looked as if someone had laid in their beds and left!

"Someone has been laying in my bed!"

"Someone has been laying in my bed!"

"Someone has been laying in my bed, and look, she's still here!!"

Golilocks was awaken upon hearing Baby Bear's shrill cries. She rose with a start, and looked around her. She was surrounded by three bears, all who looked very upset with her. She realized that this must be their house, and she had invaded their space without their permission! Oh no, she would be in so much trouble! But before she had a chance to say anything, Papa Bear rose his arms and let out a large roar. Golidlocks, frightened to the core by the roar, jumped from Baby Bear's bed and leaped out the open window in the bedroom. Once she landed outside, she ran as fast as she could, in hopes of finding her way out of the forest.

The end
