Goldilocks was a little girl who was meandering through the forest as she was running an errand for her mother. However, while she was traveling through the vast greenery, she found herself lost. Which way was she to go again? She could not remember! However, in her panic to find her way back home she suddenly found herself facing a small but cozy looking home. Curiousity taking over her, Goldilocks found herself walking up to the window and looking inside. She saw no one in the house, so she thought to herself, "Well, it wouldn't be so bad if I just had a little look around myself," because she was not a well-raised little girl. So, she turned the door handle and wandered on inside the bear's residence.

Once inside, Goldilocks was greeted by three bowls of porridge sitting on the table in front of her. "How delicious!" she thought to herself, "porridge is my favorite!" She set about helping herself to the bear's breakfast.

After picking her porridge The Bear's three chairs caught her eyes so she decided to sit down to finish eating her porridge.

Goldilocks felt satisfied, having a full stomach and resting in a very comfortable chair. However, as she leaned back to stretch her arms, she heard something snap, and all of a sudden Goldilocks found herself on the ground surrounded by pieces of a now broken chair.

"Oh that does it!" Goldilocks whined. "Nothing's gonna stop me from getting the rest what I want! Not even a broken chair!"

With a big huff, Goldilocks found herself in search of a new place of comfort: a nice, comfy bed. She climbed the stairs to the upper bedroom where she found herself faced with three different beds. "Ah, I'm super tired, I'm sure whoever lives here won't mind if I lie down real quick for a little nap.

In fact, Baby Bear's bed was so comfortable that Goldilocks found herself dozing off. Before she knew it, she was fast asleep.

But the bears had just gotten back from their walk in the woods...
